Refayat's Neighborhood

One of his favorite recipe: Basil Cappelini

Ingredients:

  • 1 large can of diced/pureed tomatoes
  • 1 small can of tomato sauce
  • 1 small can/tube of tomato paste
  • 1/2 an onion diced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper diced
  • 5 cloves of garlic thinly sliced
  • 4 table spoons of olive oil
  • Garlic salt to taste
  • 2 table spoons of sugar
  • 1 cup of fresh basil finely chopped
  • 3 table spoons of unsalted butter
  • 1 packet of capellini pasta

Preparation:

  1. Pour in the olive oil on a large saucepan and heat on medium
  2. Cook the jalapenos, onions, and garlic until onions turns translucent
  3. Pour in the diced/pureed tomatoes, sauce, and paste
  4. Continue stirring and add the chopped basil
  5. Add garlic salt and sugar for taste and continue stirring
  6. Cook on medium heat and stir frequently for 30 minutes
  7. Reduce heat to simmer and stir frequently for another 45 minutes
  8. Boil 3 quarts of water and cook the capellini pasta for 3 minutes
  9. Pour out the water and wash pasta in a collander with cold water for 3 minutes
  10. Heat the pot used to boil the pasta to medium then add the butter
  11. Add pasta and stir around making sure the butter is spread throughout
  12. After stirring around for 2 minutes turn off heat
  13. Serve pasta with the sauce and garnish with full unchopped basil leaves
